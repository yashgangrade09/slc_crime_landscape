Aggregated Statistics

Aggregated statistics for the selected crimes. The monthly, weekly and hourly statistics are computed for the currently selected year.
A flat zero line in the graph means data for that aspect is not available.

Submitted by Rebeka Mukherjee, Archit Rathore and Yash Gangrade as part of CS 6630 Visualization for Data Science, School of Computing, University of Utah.
